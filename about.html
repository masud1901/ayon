I am a machine learning researcher, currently pursuing my M.Sc. at the Institute of Information Technology, Jahangirnagar University. My work is centered on building reliable, data-efficient, and trustworthy AI systems, with a deep focus on computer vision, federated learning, and quantum machine learning.

Under the guidance of Dr. M. Hasan, PhD (VCU), I am investigating communication efficiency and trustworthiness in federated learning for my master's thesis. This research explores adaptive scheduling for heterogeneous data environments and analyzes the critical trade-off between differential privacy and robustness against adversarial attacks. I am also exploring financial modeling with Dr. K.M. Zahidul Islam.

My research journey began with computer vision, developing the MosQNet-SA project under Dr. Shahidul Islam. I've since expanded into quantum ML and graph contrastive learning through collaborations with Md. Abrar Jahin. My work in healthcare AI continues with projects in ECG analysis and Alzheimer's disease detection with Dr. Mohammad Abu Yousuf.

Beyond research, I am passionate about applying data to solve real-world problems. This passion extends to my work at the startup Nimbus Research Bureau (NRB), where I translate raw data into strategic insights. As an ex-cadet of Sylhet Cadet College, I was always fond of different sports and cultural activities, a passion I continued at university. This hands-on leadership is further honed by my role as President of the IIT-JU Sports Club and past positions at the IEEE Student Branch and E-Business & Entrepreneurship Club.

I am always open to connecting with fellow researchers and practitioners. Let's collaborate on building the next generation of impactful AI solutions.
